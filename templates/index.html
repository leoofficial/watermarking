UNIQUE is a copyright infringement protection platform based on digital watermarking techniques.

Embedding a watermark into your picture makes the picture carry your watermark forever - nobody else can claim that the author is someone else.

Extracting a watermark from the picture enables you to get the watermark embedded in the picture and verify the author of it.

UNIQUE also detects duplicate pictures uploaded to the system. Nobody can claim his or her authorship over the picture as long as you are the first one to upload it.
